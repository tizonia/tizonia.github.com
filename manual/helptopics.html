Help Topics¶

Tizonia includes a fairly comprehensive help system with information about general usage, included streaming services, configuration elements, and command-line options supported.

To obtain a list of the available help topics, just invoke tizonia --help and to retrieve information about a particular topic, use tizonia --help <topic>.

EXAMPLES¶

$ tizonia --help
